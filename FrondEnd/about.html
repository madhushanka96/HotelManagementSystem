ABOUT US


Our story begins in the early 1970s, a tale full of determination and daring, not to mention a sense of adventure! It was during this time that the late Mr. Atul Panabokke, a tea planter by profession, had the singular vision of offering travellers a true home away from home in the hill country. From humble beginnings as a four-roomed guesthouse, overseen by Mr. Panabokke and his wife and sons, the property has been transformed into the five star luxury hotel in Kandy it is today.

Throughout the passing of time, Hill Sight Royal Hotel has stayed true to its founderâ€™s original vision, creating a modern establishment rooted in rich traditions. The genuine essence of heartfelt Sri Lankan hospitality and personalized service, treating each guest as part of our extended family, is very much a part of our ethos. This indigenous identity is something that is important to us as we combine the proud heritage of our past with a sense of contemporary sophistication, providing guests with a holiday of a lifetime. The renowned Universal Group of Maldives is also now a major stakeholder of Hill Sight and this strategic alliance has started a new chapter in our story.
